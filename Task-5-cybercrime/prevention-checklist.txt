Stay Safe Online: A Guide for College Students

As a Digital Ambassador, I've mention below prevention's checklist that one should consider.
Prevention

Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
Always verify payment requests before approving them.
Do not scan QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
Avoid sharing personal or banking details over phone calls or messages.
Do not install screen-sharing apps when instructed by unknown persons.
Enable transaction alerts and regularly monitor bank statements.
Use official apps and contact verified customer care only.
Immediately report suspicious activity to your bank or the National Cyber Crime Portal.

How to Report Cybercrime in India:

If you or someone you know falls victim to a digital crime, act immediately:

National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

National Helpline Number: 1930 (Call this immediately for financial frauds).
